Welcome to bluepetal

Your one stop shop for products that are unique & hand-painted in different forms like Warli, Madhubani, Gond, abstract and floral. A wide range of hand-painted apparels like kurtis, stoles, dupattas and sarees are available. We also have a range of dining table linen, cushion covers and beautiful frames for an elegant home decor. Each of our products is unique and hand-painted, unlike factory made products that have hundreds of copies of the same design.
